In Notre Dame lore they are known as four legendary football players. For us, they are something different: creativity, initiative, risk-taking, and business acumen. They form the crest of entrepreneurship that will sweep across Notre Dame.


Notre Dame students and alumni aspire to change the world. Our founder himself predicted that this university would be a powerful means for good. We are building a strong community and support for a significant way to do so - entrepreneurship - for every ND student and alumni who has an idea and the courage to pursue it.

We’re connecting people

who have the drive to create something utterly new. Instead of scattered individuals and under-utilized resources, we’re aligning the arrows.
